万能金句

  1. 随着..的流行程度/普及程度的快速增长，我们的生活质量也被显著改变.

    With the rapidly growing popularity of …, the quality of our lives has been considerably changed.

  2. 最近,…已经被带入广泛大众视野之中/成为关注的焦点.

    Recently, sth/the problem of … has been brought to popular attention/ has become the focus of public concern.

  3. 随着…的发展，越来越多的人开始意识到.

    With the development of …, more and more people come to realize that…

  4. 在..的眼中，…看起来/意味着…

    To sb’s mind/in sb’s eyes,sth. seems /means …

  5. 现在…被广泛相信.

    Now it is widely believed that…

  6. …已经改变了我们社会的发展方式，但它光明的一面不应阻碍我们密切关注其黑暗的一面.

    Sth. has changed the way our society develops, but its bright side should not keep us from following closely its dark side.

  7. 如果没有…,我们的社会将会是怎样？

    What would our society be like if ther were no …?

  8. 在过去的几年中，…经历了一个大繁荣时期/急速增长/下降.

    In the few years, there has been a boom/sharp growth/decline in …

  9. 在我们的生活中，在…时经常会出现这样的情况.

    In our life, there often appears such an occasion when …/on which..
